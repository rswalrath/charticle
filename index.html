LGBT Youth Statistics

A charticle on the lesbian, gay, bisexual and
transgender youth population of America.

8 of 10

students have been verbally harassed, and 4 of 10 physically harassed at school.

71.3%

of students hear homophobic slurs frequently.

80%

of transgender students reported feeling unsafe at school because of their gender expression.

3X

LGB students are up to three times more likely
to experience harsh disciplinary treatment
by school administrators than their non-LGB
peers.

33%

of LGBT students report that school officials
fail to address harassment, which causes distrust
of school administrators.

LGBT youth make up between 5% and 7% of the total population but represent 15% of those in the juvenile justice system.

40%

of homelessness youth are LGBT.

1 in 3

transgender youth will be turned away from a shelter due to their gender identity or expression.

19%

of transgender people experience homelessness.

4X

LGB youth are four times more likely to attempt suicide as their straight peers.

1/2

Nearly half of young transgender people have seriously thought about taking their lives, and one-quarter report having made a suicide attempt.

8.4X

LGB youth who come from highly rejecting families are 8.4 times as likely to have attempted suicide as LGB peers who reported no or low levels of family rejection.
