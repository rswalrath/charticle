LGBT Youth Statistics

A charticle on the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender
youth population (ages 10-18) of America.

80%

Eight in 10 students have been verbally harassed, and four in 10 physically harassed, at school.

71.3%

Nearly three-quarters of students hear homophobic slurs frequently.

80%

Eight in 10 transgender students reported feeling unsafe at school because of their gender expression.

3X

LGB students are up to three times more likely to experience harsh disciplinary treatment by school administrators than their non-LGB peers.

33%

One-third of LGBT students report that school officials fail to address harassment, which causes distrust of school administrators.

LGBT youth make up between 5 and 7 percent of the total population but represent 15 percent of those in the juvenile justice system.

40%

Four in 10 homeless youths are LGBT.

19%

Nearly one-fifth of all transgender people experience homelessness at some point in their lives.

33%

One in three transgender youth will be turned away from a shelter due to their gender identity or expression.

4X

LGB youth are four times more likely to attempt suicide than their straight peers.

50%

Nearly half of young transgender people have seriously thought about taking their lives, and one-quarter report having made a suicide attempt.

8.4X

LGB youth who come from highly rejecting families are 8.4 times as likely to have attempted suicide as LGB peers who reported no or low levels of family rejection.
